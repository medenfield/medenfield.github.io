Building transactional email workflows with Drupal and Mandrill

Posted on April 20, 2017

At Open Angler, transactional texts and emails are an important part of how we facilitate and improve upon the process of booking a charter boat or fishing trip online. We use Mandrill to manage our entire suite of outbound email to customers and charter providers, gaining insight into bounce and open confirmation, and empowering our content team to make trivial changes to email templates without developer intervention. Most web application frameworks have built in or readily available modules to handle sending email, but plaintext is likely the default, and it’s likely done from the same server you host your website without any tools for greater insight.
